Residential Property Maintenance

Comprehensive care for residential properties.

CBM delivers full-spectrum planned and reactive maintenance for residential developments, housing associations, and private landlords across Edinburgh and the Lothians. We act as a trusted extension of your property management team.

Whether you need a full planned preventive maintenance programme or fast reactive response to an urgent repair, our experienced operatives are equipped and ready.

  • Planned Preventive Maintenance (PPM) programmes
  • Reactive maintenance and emergency call-out
  • Roofing, guttering and external envelope repairs
  • Plumbing, heating and hot water systems
  • Electrical installation and periodic inspection
  • Communal area upkeep and redecoration

Domestic Services

Professional home care, delivered with care.

For homeowners and private tenants across Edinburgh and the Lothians, CBM offers professional, reliable home maintenance services delivered with a commitment to quality, safety, and respect for your property.

Our domestic team handles everything from a dripping tap to a full bathroom installation — always on time, always tidy, and always backed by our commitment to exceptional service.

04 · Facilities

Facilities Management

Protecting asset value through proactive management.

CBM's facilities management service provides building owners and occupiers with a single, accountable partner for all maintenance needs — preserving asset value, ensuring compliance, and keeping operations running without interruption.

We work with you to build a bespoke FM contract — combining planned maintenance, reactive response, and compliance reporting into one clear, cost-effective service.

  • Full FM contracts — single or multi-site
  • PPM schedules tailored to your asset register
  • 24/7 reactive maintenance response capability
  • Statutory compliance management (EICR, Gas, LOLER)
  • Dedicated account manager and monthly reporting
  • Lifecycle planning and capital works advice
