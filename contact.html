Common Questions

Frequently asked.

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Call us directly on +44 7842 976886 or email info@cbmconstruction.co.uk.

What areas do CBM cover?

CBM serves Edinburgh, East Lothian, Midlothian, West Lothian, and the wider Lothians area. For larger commercial or FM contracts, we can also cover broader parts of central Scotland — please contact us to discuss.

Are your operatives fully qualified and insured?

Yes. All CBM operatives hold the relevant qualifications for their discipline — including Gas Safe registration, NICEIC approval, and industry-standard certifications. We carry full public liability and employers' liability insurance on every project.

How quickly can you respond to a reactive maintenance call?

For clients on a facilities management contract, we offer a 24/7 reactive response service. For non-contract reactive work, we aim to attend within 24–48 hours for urgent issues and provide a confirmed appointment date within one working day of your enquiry.

Do you provide free quotes?

Yes — we provide free, no-obligation quotes for all projects. For smaller domestic jobs we can often quote over the phone or via email. For larger projects we'll arrange a site visit at a time that suits you.

Can CBM work in occupied properties?

Absolutely. We regularly carry out works in occupied residential and commercial properties and understand the importance of minimising disruption, maintaining cleanliness and communicating clearly with occupants throughout.
